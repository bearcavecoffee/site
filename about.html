About Us

Bear Cave Coffee is a coffee shop located in downtown Mesquite, Texas. Founding the coffee shop in November of 2018 was our opportunity to merge two of our greatest passions: crafting delicious coffee beverages, and giving back to the community in Mesquite. We have extended our love of the community to our business practices as well. Here at Bear Cave Coffee, we source all of our coffee from Cultivar Coffee Roasting Co. in Dallas, Texas.

Bear Cave Coffee has firmly established itself at the Corner Theatre in downtown Mesquite. This location has given us the opportunity to satisfy everyone's caffeine craving from a spot that places us right in the middle of all the warmth and love this town has to offer. Our biggest hope is that we can give as much back to this town as it has given us throughout our lives.
