About

Bear Cave Coffee is a small shop in the heart of downtown Mesquite, Texas. We value the beauty that art and music can bring to a community. We love bringing people together, whether it be for a great cause, or just because it is a wonderful thing. We, along with the Corner Theatre, bring monthly events to the shop to enjoy and cultivate relationships centered around the likeness of coffee.
